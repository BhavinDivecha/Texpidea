Texpidea Technologies PVT.LTD.

Texpidea established in 2022 provides intelligent 2D and 3D Fabric draping solutions, to help Textile and Fashion companies to boost the sales and customer satisfaction, by using creativity & 3D visualization tools to improve Sales, Design requirements.

Texpidea came into to picture to develop specialized software applications for the fashion and garment industry. It is our constant endeavor to create software solutions that encourage users to work the way they think.

We now have a wide range of software solutions for the fashion and garment industry. Our software applications run on the very affordable Hand to hand android mobile. Our products require little or no learning to get hands-on which helps us to reach the maximum.
